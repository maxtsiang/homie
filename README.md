# Homie

A student sublease platform.

## Description

Connect people looking to sublease their space to verified university students! For subletters, homie lets you enforce processes like automatic rent payments to ensure on time payments. For students, homie lets you find competitive prices of spaces with powerful map-based search and filter capabilities. Homie also lets you connect with potential housemates who are also interested in certain sublease listings and allows you to save prices by renting together!
